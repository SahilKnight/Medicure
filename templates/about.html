About Us

Empowering Health with AI

PharmaLane is a cutting-edge medical recommendation platform that combines machine learning with real doctor consultations to make healthcare accessible to everyone.

Our Vision

A world where access to healthcare information is a fundamental right, not a luxury. We empower individuals with the knowledge and tools to take control of their health.

Our Mission

To provide a seamless, AI-powered platform that helps identify potential health concerns based on symptoms, while offering educational resources to enhance health literacy.

How We Do It

Our platform uses a robust SVM model trained on thousands of symptom-disease pairs, enhanced with AI insights and connected to real doctors for video consultations.

Disclaimer
PharmaLane is designed for informational purposes only. It does not replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider for medical decisions.
